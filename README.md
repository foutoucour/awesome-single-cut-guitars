## Price Range Guide

The price ranges use a 6-tier system represented by money emojis (💰):
(in USD)

- 💰 = Budget (Under $500)
- 💰💰 = Entry-Level ($500 - $1,000)
- 💰💰💰 = Mid-Range ($1,000 - $2,000)
- 💰💰💰💰 = Premium ($2,000 - $4,000)
- 💰💰💰💰💰 = High-End ($4,000 - $8,000)
- 💰💰💰💰💰💰 = Ultra-Premium ($8,000+)

> **Note**  
> Prices are approximate and based on the retail prices of new guitars. Used guitars, regional variations, and specific
> models may vary significantly from these ranges.
