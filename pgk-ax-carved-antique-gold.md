## Finishing — Order of Operations

1. **Final sanding** — 150 → 220 → 320, full body + neck
2. **Grain filler on the top** — apply cross-grain, dry fully, sand back to 220
3. **TransTint Golden Amber on the top** — cloth application, let penetrate, light sand at 400
4. **Laser engrave your logo** on the ebony headstock overlay — do this BEFORE any Tru-Oil
5. **Tru-Oil coats 1–6** — thin wipe-on coats, 0000 steel wool between each after coat 3 · Sand at 400 between first
   coats
6. **Tru-Oil + mica on top only** (optional) — pinch of Pearl Ex in the Tru-Oil, coats 4–6
7. **Tru-Oil coats 7–12** — sand at 600 (not 400) between coats · Building gloss depth
8. **Tru-Oil coats 13–15** — dilute ~10% with mineral oil for ultra-thin final coats · No sanding between these
9. **Cure — minimum 2 weeks** before polishing · Tru-Oil continues hardening long after it feels dry · Do not skip
10. **Wet sand** — 800 → 1000 → 1500 → 2000 wet/dry paper with water · Level the surface completely
11. **Meguiar's Ultimate Compound** by hand with microfibre — removes 2000-grit scratches · Work in circular motion
12. **Meguiar's Ultimate Polish** with clean microfibre — final gloss stage · Reveals deep vintage 3D depth in the flame
    maple
